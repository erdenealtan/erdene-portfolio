About

The intersection of data,
evidence & impact

Through his background in Public Policy at KU Leuven, Erdene developed a passion for statistics, indicators, and evidence-based analysis — and a strong belief that good monitoring and evaluation is what turns development intentions into measurable impact.

Currently on assignment at Nike as a Product Analyst, Erdene designs and maintains indicator and reporting frameworks, runs data quality assurance processes, and translates operational data into structured insights for stakeholders — core monitoring & evaluation functions, currently applied in a corporate operations context.

Born in Mongolia and shaped by the Flemish education system in Belgium, Erdene brings a combination of analytical rigour from European consulting and cultural fluency in Mongolia. His KU Leuven master's thesis — awarded magna cum laude — applied a monitoring & evaluation lens to Mongolia's progress on the UN Sustainable Development Goals. That work reflects the direction Erdene is building toward: contributing to monitoring & evaluation in international development, starting in Belgium with Mongolia as a long-term focus.
