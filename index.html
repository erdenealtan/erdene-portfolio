About

The intersection of data,
policy & cultural fluency

Through his background in Public Policy at KU Leuven, Erdene developed a passion for statistics and working with data — and an avid belief in data-driven decision making as a force for meaningful change.

Currently on assignment at Nike as a Product Analyst, Erdene operates at the intersect of technology and business — driving data-informed decisions to fuel product innovation and operational efficiency across EMEA. His core focus is translating complex data into actionable insights that empower business stakeholders to leverage technology for competitive advantage.

Born in Mongolia and shaped by the Flemish education system in Belgium, Erdene brings a rare combination: the analytical rigour of European consulting and an intimate cultural and linguistic fluency in Mongolia. His KU Leuven master's thesis — awarded magna cum laude — modelled Mongolia's progress toward the UN Sustainable Development Goals. That research was a personal commitment, not just an academic one.
